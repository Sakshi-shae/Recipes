Meetha Churma recipe

Ingredients Required for Roti ka Meetha Churma

  • Whole wheat flour .
  • Pure Desi Ghee.
  • Powdered sugar or crushed jaggery
  • Milk (in small quantity)
  • Chopped Almonds

The process to Make Haryanvi Roti Ka Meetha Churma

  1. Make a dough for makinf a chapati
  2. Divide the dough into small sized balls and roll them into flat chapatis.After that, put the hot chapatis in a big size plate and crush them into small pieces.
  3. On medium flame roast the chapati
  4. Pour 2 or 3 tablespoons of Desi Ghee and add Powdered sugar or jaggery to the mixture.
  5. Crush the mixture well with hands and give it the shape of round laddoos.
  6. Now, this traditional delicacy Haryanvi Roti Ka Meetha Churma is ready to relish.
